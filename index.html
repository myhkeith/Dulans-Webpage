About the Business

Dulan's Soul Food is a family-and-friend favorite of both native Angelenos and tourists alike. With 3 locations in Inglewood and the heart of South-Central Los Angeles, our food is a representation of rich culture, rich nourishment, and rich flavor. Established in 1999, Dulan's serves food that is reminiscent of your grandmother's on a Sunday evening after church service. With protein ranging from chicken, cooked a vareity of ways, to Ox Tails to a vegetarian option, there's bound to be an option that matches to your tastes. Come on down to one of our 3 locations today and try our food, both from the soul and for the soul!

Plates

Fried Chicken - Mac and Cheese - Yams

A tried and true classic combination in the world of soul food. Juicy fried chicken matched one of the most well-known power couples, Mac and Cheese and Yams. A food hack, known by many, is to eat both the sweet Yams and Mac and Cheese in the same bite, but beware... you might come running back in line for more!

Meatloaf - Black-Eyed peas - Mashed Potatoes

A tender, savory meatloaf accompanied by creamy mashed potatoes and black-eyed peas. This delectable combination of classic dishes brings together the essence of Southern comfort, providing a true taste of home.

Baked Fish - Beans - Mixed Vegetables

A wholesome and nutritious meal with a mouthwatering baked fish, seasoned to perfection and cooked until it's flaky and tender. Complementing the fish is a side of protein-rich beans and a colorful medley of mixed vegetables. This balanced and delicious combination offers a delightful dining experience while keeping your health and well-being in mind. (My personal favorite)
